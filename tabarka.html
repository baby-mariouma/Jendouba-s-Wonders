About Tabarka

Tabarka is a coastal town known for its pristine beaches, coral reefs, and the famous rock formations known as Les Aiguilles (The Needles). It is a hub for diving, music festivals, and outdoor activities.

Top Attractions

  • Les Aiguilles
  • Tabarka Marina
  • Fort Genoese (Tabarka Castle)
  • Coral Museum
  • Tabarka Beach
