About Ain Draham

Ain Draham is a picturesque mountain town famous for its cedar forests, cool climate, and charming architecture. It is a top destination for winter visitors due to its snow-covered scenery.

Activities to Enjoy

  • Hiking in the forests
  • Exploring traditional houses
  • Horseback riding
  • Snow activities (winter)

Weather & Activities
