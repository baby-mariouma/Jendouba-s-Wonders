About Bulla Regia

Bulla Regia is an archaeological site of great historical importance, showcasing the unique architecture of underground Roman villas. This well-preserved site offers a glimpse into ancient Roman life in Tunisia, with its intricate mosaics, theaters, and baths.

Highlights of Bulla Regia

  • The underground Roman villas
  • Beautifully preserved mosaics
  • The ancient Roman theater
  • Historical public baths
  • A unique blend of Berber and Roman heritage
